Cookie Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Incredible India Explorer uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience, enhance security, and understand application usage.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you interact with our features, and provide a secure browsing experience.

Cookie Categories

Category Purpose Required
Essential Core application functionality ✅
Authentication User sign-in/session management ✅
Analytics Improve product usage insights Optional
Preferences Remember user settings Optional
Third-Party Firebase/Vercel integrations Depends

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for Incredible India Explorer to function. They enable core features like navigating between pages and accessing secure areas of the application. Without these cookies, certain services cannot be provided.

Authentication Cookies

If you use Firebase Authentication to log into Incredible India Explorer, we use cookies to maintain your session securely. This ensures that you do not have to repeatedly sign in as you move between different pages.

Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies, provided by services like Firebase Analytics or Vercel Analytics, help us understand how visitors interact with the application. We use this information to improve our features, performance, and overall user experience.

Preference Cookies

These cookies allow our application to remember choices you make, such as your preferred theme (e.g., light or dark mode) or language preferences. This provides a more personalized experience.

Third-Party Cookies

In some cases, third-party services we integrate with (such as mapping services or embedded content) may place cookies on your device. We do not have direct control over these cookies and recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. However, please note that disabling essential or authentication cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of Incredible India Explorer.

Cookie Retention

Cookies remain on your device for varying periods. Session cookies are deleted automatically when you close your browser, while persistent cookies remain until they expire or you manually delete them.

Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy occasionally to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or our operations. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any updates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I disable cookies?

Yes. Most browsers allow you to manage or disable cookies, though doing so may affect some features.

Which cookies are required?

Essential and authentication cookies are required for secure operation.

Do you use tracking cookies?

Only if analytics services such as Firebase Analytics or Vercel Analytics are enabled.
