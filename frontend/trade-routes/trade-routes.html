Indo-Roman Trade Boom

Following the discovery of the monsoon winds by Greek navigator Hippalus, Roman fleets sailed directly across the Arabian Sea. Up to 120 Roman ships sailed annually from Egyptian Red Sea ports to Muziris, bringing massive flows of gold.
